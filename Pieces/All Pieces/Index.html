Ethan Marcotte

Ethan had a very large impact on the way the web is designed. He completely changed the way sites are created and displayed with a whole new concept known as “Responsive Design”. Responsive design is the process of altering a site through media queries to be supported by other devices that may not be just a monitor.

"The web isn’t a “platform” like a native OS: it’s a continuum. Varying support levels work with progressive enhancement."
